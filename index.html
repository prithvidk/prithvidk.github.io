SAFE WATCH

SafeWatch addresses the challenge of monitoring young children using deep learning and computer vision. Our system identifies and tracks facial features and skeletal movements to detect behaviors during sleep and wakefulness. MediaPipe's real-time facial landmark and body pose tools extract features from video streams, which are then classified by the LSTM model into behaviors such as sleep, wakefulness, and movement patterns. This system enhances child safety by providing real-time monitoring.
